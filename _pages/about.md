[Lifu Tu](https://lifu-tu.github.io/) is a research scientist at Salesforce AI Research. He earned his Ph.D. from [TTI-Chicago](https://ttic.edu/), a philanthropically endowed academic computer science institute located on the [University of Chicago](https://www.uchicago.edu/) campus.

His current research focus is on natural language processing and deep learning, especially energy-based models and approximated inference networks. He is also doing some work on model robustness and representation learning.
